Unit 05 · Nudge Plan

Design interventions that make change easier

Use the EAST framework. Each nudge should respond to a specific diagnosed barrier — not be chosen at random. The question is always: what makes this next step easier, more appealing, more social, or better timed?

Easy
Reduce friction
Simplify the desired behaviour. Defaults, templates, one-click access, pre-filled forms. Make doing nothing harder than changing.
Attractive
Make it appealing
Personalise the benefit. Use recognition, visible progress, or identity alignment. People move toward things that feel rewarding.
Social
Use peer influence
Show that people similar to them are already doing it. Champions, peer stories, visible adoption by respected colleagues.
Timely
Intervene at the right moment
Deliver when people are most receptive — at a decision point, after a frustrating experience, or when momentum is building.
